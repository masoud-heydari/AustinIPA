Austin's First Iranian 501(c) Organization

The Austin Iranian Professionals Association (AustinIPA) is proud to be Austin's first Iranian nonprofit cultural organization. We bring together Iranian professionals, students, and community members to celebrate our rich heritage while contributing to the vibrant Austin community.

Through cultural events, professional networking, and community service, we create meaningful connections and preserve the beautiful traditions of Persian culture for future generations.
